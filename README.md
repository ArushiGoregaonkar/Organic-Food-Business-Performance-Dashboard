# 🌿 Organic Food Business Performance Dashboard

This project is a **comprehensive Business Intelligence Dashboard** built to analyze and visualize the performance of an **Organic Food business**. It provides meaningful insights into sales, revenue, profit, inventory, demand, and regional performance through interactive and visually appealing charts.

The dashboard converts raw business data into **actionable insights**, helping in better decision-making, performance tracking, and business growth strategies.

---

# 📊 Project Overview

In today’s data-driven world, businesses need quick and clear insights to understand their performance. This dashboard was created to solve that problem by presenting key business metrics in a structured and easy-to-understand format.

It focuses on analyzing:

* Sales performance across products and categories
* Revenue trends over time
* Profit distribution by category
* Inventory and demand balance
* State-wise sales performance in India
* Operational issues like supply delay and low stock
